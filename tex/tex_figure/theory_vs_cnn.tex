As the noise level $\sigma$ increases, the CNN outputs exhibit a closer match to the analytical solutions, indicating that the network begins to learn the analytical formula once $\sigma$ reaches certain threshold. The choice of the pre-inverse operator $B$ play a critical role: in the inpainting task, setting $B = A^+$ yields better performance, whereas in the deconvolution task, selecting $B = \Id$ leads to better results.}
